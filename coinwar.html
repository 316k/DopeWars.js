In late 2022 omicron variant b.9 erradicated over 75% of the world's population, which resulted in complete collapse of all governments and financial institutions. All fiat currency devalued and multiple pop-up governments established themselves on existing and new cryptocurrencies. These proved nearly as unstable as fiat and required constant switching between exchanges as prices fluctuated. TL;DR

Drugwars was a turn-based strategy computer game in which the player assumes the role of a drug dealer engaged in arbitrage. The object of the game is to deal the most drugs to pay off the "loan shark" (who charges interest) by the end of the game and make a profit. Inspired by the text-based BASIC game Star Trader, Drugwars was originally an MS-DOS program created by John E. Dell in 1984. Dope Wars for Windows, an adaption of the original DOS source code, was available from 1998 to early 2005 and was listed in the "50 Most Popular Games" for over 6 years. Beermat Software has since released native versions for mobile as well.
